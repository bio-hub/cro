Molecular signatures associated with the development of pulmonary toxicities in mesothelioma patients treated with radical hemithoracic radiation therapy

Sergio Crovella1, Alberto Revelant2, Elena Muraro3, Ronald Rodrigues Moura4, Lucas Brandão4, Marco Trovò5, Agostino Steffan6, Paola Zacchi7, Giuliano Zabucchi7, Emilio Minatel2, Violetta Borelli7

Star

View supplementary data

Article
Goals

Contributes to the identification of a possible role for genetic variations in the response to radiotherapy in MPM patients, we performed whole exome sequencing, coupled with a novel bioinformatic approach, to identify not only the genetic variants associated to pulmonary toxicity after radiotherapy, but also to detect potential biological pathways that can better elucidate the observed phenotype.
